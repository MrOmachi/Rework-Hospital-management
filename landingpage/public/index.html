Digital banking for global African businesses

Accept payments in multiple currencies, convert currencies with one click, transfer locally or across borders, and spend with a card

For: Startups | Traders | SMEs | Freelancers

Use Cases

Accept Investments

Open a free USD (not domiciliary accounts) and NGN bank account to accept payments from your customers and investors. Simply share your bank account details with your customers and receive payments for free.

Convert Currencies

Transfer money from your Cleva account to any bank account in Nigeria or in the US. Pay your vendors and suppliers with ease no matter where they’re located.

Create Virtual Cards

Don’t mix business spend with personal spend. Create virtual and physical cards in USD or NGN, fund the card with the amount you want, assign the card to your employees, and track spend.

Track Finances

Easily view how much money your business has received and spent over a period of time. No more spreadsheets, use Cleva!
